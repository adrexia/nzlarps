Shadow and Light

This day game ran on Sunday 19th September 2004. See the forum for feedback about the game.

Word has spread like wildfire of a wedding between the vampire Baron Eric von Braun, lord of Dareen and the demoness Gethsemane. Dareen, a northern forest village known for its sour faced villagers and occasional bandit attacks is the location of the wedding.

All nobles are invited be they Kings, Princes, Dukes, Barons or minor nobility. Commoners are welcome to come but should they desire entry to the feast they had best ask the Lords permission or come as part of a noble's retinue. Bonded slaves are welcome, outlaws are not.

A feast will be provided for all invited guests. Guests are advised to bring arms as a precaution, though bearing them into the great hall is not advised. Daggers are freely permitted.

Event Details

Time Sunday 19th September, from 5:00pm (crew will be needed from 3pm)
Venue Camp Sladdin, Thorpes Quarry Rd, Clevedon, Auckland
Directions On the Southern Motorway, take the Papakura off ramp. Drive towards Ardmore Airport so you pass it on the south side. This is the Papakura-Clevedon Road. Stay on this road and you will reach Clevedon after a 15-20 minute drive. Thorpes Quarry Rd is on your left just outside Clevedon.
Cost $15 for players, $10 for crew (includes feast, bring a medieval dish for a $3 discount)
Organiser Alan Grant. Email A.Grant@telecom.co.nz to book.
What to Bring Your character's costume and equipment. Finery is encouraged, but parts of the game may take part outdoors so please wear suitable footwear and avoid costumes that trail on the ground.
Notes Alan is organising bookings, venue and food. However, he will be a player at the event. The GMs are Carl Sigvertsen and Alex Herbert.
Risk & Reward This game is rated as having Low risk. That's safer than a weekend game, but combat is still likely and death is possible.
