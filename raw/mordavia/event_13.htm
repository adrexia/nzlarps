Meanwhile, Back In Faerie

The Honourable Society of Key Wardens (the notorious Unseelie drinking club) is having its annual bash at the Lair of the Tunnel Web Spider. It is renowned both for its interesting guests from all over Faerie and for the quality of its booze. There might even be Royalty this year. This is also their main recruiting drive and YOU have been invited. Expect drinking, scheming, music and dancing.

Please Note:
This game will last about an hour and is set in the Realm of Faerie. Drinks and light snacks will be provided.

While this game takes place in the existing Mordavia campaign, it is for Fairy characters only. People are welcome to come as existing characters, make new characters or try one of our fine selection of pre-written characters.

People who have never played a Mordavia game before are very welcome and are recommended to come as a pre-written character. We will supply costumes for anyone who does not wish to bring their own. Please let us know if you will need a costume.

Event Details

Time Saturday, 26 March 2005, 9 pm.
Booking Deadline Midday, Saturday, 26 March. There is space for 20 people, first paid, first booked.
Venue ICON Science Fiction Convention, WestPlaza Hotel, Wellington. Icon's Website.
Cost $10 for members of the convention, $15 for non-members, please pay the Convention.
Organisers Stephanie Pegg and Catherine Pegg
Contact Stephanie Pegg, (04) 977 2989, spegg@derceto.com for LARP queries.
Lynelle Howell, enquiries@icon.sf.org.nz for ICON queries.
What to Bring Your character's costume if you don't want to be dressed from our wardrobe. Any spare costume that you're willing to lend.
Risk & Reward This game is rated as having Low risk. Most of the action will be social, combat and death are possible but not very likely.
