Death of a Merchant

William Löwe was the owner of the "Eranov Exporters" guild house. He was a wealthy and widely travelled merchant, a purveyor of all kinds of goods. You knew him well. You were his family, his friends, his business colleages. Now he is dead, murdered by persons unknown on the road between Borsa and Dareen. William’s funeral will be held in the forested hills he loved, west of Eranov, as specified in his will.

Mordavia: Death of a Merchant is a 1-hour Mordavia scenario that will run at BattleCry 2005. Both newcomers and old hands are invited to play. Create a character that has a reason to be at William Löwe's funeral and the reading of his will. Feel free to invent any details to flesh out your association with him, nothing is off limits. Submit your character concept to ryan@mordavia.com, or come see me before the game to discuss your character. We'll take all the concepts and make them consistent, then use them as inspiration for writing the game scenario.
