Winterspite

This day game was played on 8th July. See discussion on the forum.

Background



Event Details

Date Saturday 8th July 2006.
Time 2pm for Crew, 4pm for players, finish 9pm, then afterlarp at Mike's.
Venue Western Springs
Crew meet at Mike Curtis' place
Players meet at the corner of Great North and Motions Roads. (Motions Rd is the road the Zoo is on).
Cost $15 for players
$5 for crew
$5 discount for NZLARPS members (i.e. members crew for free)
Organisers Mike Curtis. Click here to send Mike a Private Message.
What to Bring Your character's costume and equipment. Some IC food will be available (at IC cost), but best to eat and drink first and bring something to eat too, as well as a mug for soup. The game is entirely outdoors and mud is likely, wear suitable clothing.
Risk & Reward This game is rated as being High risk. Character death is not unlikely.
