The Candle

Mordavia: The Candle was played on 23rd August 2006. Discussion about the event can be found in the forum.

Background

Jurawicca has fallen to the Swamp. The Eranov dykes have been joined just north of the town of Lunca. Eranov and its farmlands are now enclosed by the Swamp on all sides, and cut off from the rest of Mordavia except by recall, which is little used since the uprising lead by Duke Featherhall. The reduced pasture can only feed a portion of the city's population, those left without are starving.

Eranov now faces the Swamp alone. Its presence overshadows the city, the stench of decay wafting over the houses when the wind is in the south, and the restless dead can be seen shambling in the mire from the outer walls. How long can the dyke, that has been stretched so long, hold back the malevolence of the Dark One's corruption?

The elven noble Megilindir has called a secret council of those who claim to work against the Dark One, to meet in his inn the Gilthanis in Eranov. There perhaps they shall light a candle as a beacon of hope for Mordavia, in a time when little hope remains.

Event Details

Time 5pm for Crew, 6pm for players, finish 9pm
Venue Mecca Stonehouse, 44 Tamaki Drive, Mission Bay.
Directions In the sea-side reserve on your left when you enter Mission Bay from the city.
Cost $20 for players
$5 for crew
$5 discount for NZLARPS members (i.e. members crew for free)
Organisers Adam. Click here to send Adam a Private Message.
What to Bring Your character's costume and equipment. Light snacks provided, recommend you eat before arriving.
Risk & Reward This game is rated as being Low risk. Character death is possible but unlikely.
Note Only characters who are invited know about this council. If you have not received an invitation from Adam (directly or indirectly as a retainer or trusted acquantance of a noble who is invited) then you will not know about the gathering so your character will not attend, unless your character is already noted as being a guest at the Gilthanis Inn in Eranov. You're welcome to attend as crew if your character cannot attend.
