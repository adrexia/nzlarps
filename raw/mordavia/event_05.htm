The Forsaken

The Forsaken was played on the 4th of April. There are photos of the game available, and see The Forsaken topic in the forums to hear what the players and crew thought.


Game Briefing

In the months since the King declared that all infants less than one year of age must die, the land has grieved. But none grieve more than the people of Hoven, who have lost both their children and their Lord, Baron Edreak von Hoven.

Five months since his death in the forest near Berium-in-the-Swamp, the ashes of von Hoven are being returned to Hoven to be preserved in the tomb of his ancestors in a state funeral. Nobles and commoners from across the land are coming to pay their last respects. You are among them.

You are finally approaching Hoven on the road through its westerly forest marches. Though your long journey is nearly over, your party is troubled. A sense of terrible foreboding chills you.

Evil has a new presence in the realm

Event Details

Time Saturday 3rd of April 2004, from 4pm to 8pm
Venue Waikowhai Park, Waikowhai
Directions Meet in the first carpark down Waikowhai Rd (off Hillsborough Rd)
Cost $10 for players, $4 for crew (money goes to props for Mordavia)
Organiser Scottie. Phone 021 211 9125 or email scott@mordavia.com to book
What to Bring Your character's costume and equipment, a change of clothing and a towel in case it's wet, a torch for mages, something to drink, something to eat, a sharp mind.
Notes Please can I have all character sheets in by the 27th of March. There are only seven openings for players, and thirteen openings for crew, so first in first served. The revised Mordavia rule system will be in place; all characters must conform to the new March 2004 Edition (not yet released) and gear must be purchased using the system in the rulebook. Existing characters can be used.
Risk & Reward This game is rated as having Medium risk. That's about the same as a weekend game (in other words, we'll be surprised if everyone survives). Characters that survive will gain some advancement points




Warming Up

Players who are booked in for The Forsaken are invited to join in a warm-up roleplay on the In Character forum. This is a good chance to get to know each other's characters before the event.
