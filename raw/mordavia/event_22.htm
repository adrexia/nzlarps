Sanctuary

The city of Eranov can no longer feed its population. The Order of Erana have made plans to evacuate a portion of the population to the deep south of Mordavia. Now the dykes around Eranov have mysteriously been damaged. Fearing that the entire city may be at risk, the Order is moving with haste to find a new home for the people.

The intended destination is the elven land of Elendar, which has not yet been touched by the swamp. With Mordavia falling and the growth of the swamp the people are in low spirits but in Elendar there seems to be a silent calm, like the breath before a deep plunge or the calm before a storm, and news is spreading of a happy occasion.

Prince Brutus, half-elven son of the King of Mordavia and former lord of Eranov, has re-emerged and has announced that he is to be married. His royal wedding will take place in Elendar and news quickly spreads that all are invited and that there will be a celebration with food being distributed to anyone who attends. Is it possible that for one night the people of Mordavia can celebrate in the midst of the struggle to save their home?

Event Details

Date Friday 22nd September 2006
Time 5pm for crew, 6:30pm for players, finish at 9am the next morning. A casual AfterLarp is planned for as soon as the game finishes and we have cleaned up.
Venue Kiwanis Huia Camp, 1322 Huia Road
Directions Take the Nth Western Motorway and exit at Hilsborough Road. Turn Left onto White Swan Road (could possibly also be called Donovan St.) Follow along this road until it becomes Kinross Street then turn left onto Godley Road and follow this for a while. Then turn left onto Titirangi Road and follow this until you get to a roundabout where you should turn Left onto Huia Road. Follow this along for ages until you pass Huia bay, the camp is on the left just past the ranger's station which is on the right. If you reach Kaiteke Point or Little Huia you have gone too far.
Cost $35 for players
$20 for crew
$5 discount for NZLARPS members
Organisers Nikki. Click here to send Nikki a Private Message.
What to Bring Your characters costume and equipment. A light meal and snacks will be provided Friday night and a simple breakfast Saturday morning. Please bring eating gear (i.e.: plate, goblet etc.). Also if you are intending to stay the night please bring sleeping gear as well. Please bring some snacks for the AfterLarp.
Risk & Reward This game is rated as Medium risk, similar to a weekend event. Some character deaths would not be surprising.
Note Unfortunately alcohol is not allowed at Kiwanis and if you are a smoker please do not smoke inside the buildings. Please let Nikki know as soon as possible if you will be attending as crew or player so that meals can be planned accordingly.
