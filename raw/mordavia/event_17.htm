The Black Woods

Mordavia: The Black Woods was played on the 8th of October 2005. See the forum for feedback about the game.

Background

Count Eric von Braun harboured a bitter hatred for the robber baron Ghita Hammerhand. Long had the Count waited for an opportunity to strike out against the man who has preyed on his people and even dared to invade the township of Dareen.

Finally that opportunity arrived. As dusk approached, the Count sets out into the Black Woods east of Dareen with a select group of followers, to hunt down the villain and bring him to justice. Striking out with eight companions into the wild lands east of Dareen, the Count faced braved a cult of vampires in the swampy Black Woods before finally laying his foe to rest despite being taken in a devious ambush.

Event Details

Start Saturday 8th October 2005, starts at 5pm (arrive at 4pm to prepare).
End Around 8pm.
Venue Chatswood Reserve, Chatswood, North Shore.
Directions Get off the Northern Motorway at Onewa Rd (just over the Harbour Bridge on the North Shore). Follow Onewa Rd until it becomes Highbury Bypass. Turn left off Highbury Bypass into Chelsea View Drive. Take the first left into Porrit Ave. Mappin Place is third on the right, park at the end of the road.
Cost $4, pay at the game
Organisers Brian Watson and Raoul Leroi. Click here to send Brian a Private Message.
What to Bring Your character's costume and equipment. Eat beforehand and bring food and a drink to get you through dinner time. The game is in a wood, so wear suitable shoes and clothes in case of mud and rain.
Risk & Reward This game is rated as being High risk. There will be hostile conflict and it's likely that some player characters will die.
